About

I am a Ph.D. candidate focusing on the development and use of computational approaches in the neurobiology field. My research focus is gene regulation of nervous system development especially the evolution of neuronal differentiation. To understand nervous system development, I am mainly using multi-omic data analysis techniques such as scRNAseq, scATACseq, and ChIPseq by extensively usage of Linux (HPC clusters and bash scripting), R, and Python. I am also Linux enthusiastic and interested in web development.
